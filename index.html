🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

What We Store Locally
Avalon AI stores the following in your browser's localStorage only: your username & hashed credentials, chat history & sessions, AI memory notes, app settings & preferences, and your API key.


What We Do NOT Collect
We do not operate any servers. No data is ever sent to the creator (NA Mhlongo) or any third party. Your conversations go directly from your browser to Anthropic's API.


Anthropic's Privacy
Your messages are processed by Anthropic's Claude API. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for how they handle API data.


Clearing Your Data
You can clear all stored data at any time using the "Reset Memory" button in the sidebar, or by clearing your browser's localStorage.
